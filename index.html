One Stitch at a Time

Hand Embroidery is the art of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to stitch thread or yarn. It may also incorporate other materials such as pearls, beads, quills, and sequins.

Thread

A long strand of material, often composed of several filaments or fibres, used for joining, or decorating textiles.

Wooden Frame

An embroidery hoop or (earlier) tambour frame consists of a pair of concentric circular or elliptical rings.

Pattern

A knitting pattern is a set of written instructions on how to construct items using knitting. Some patterns mix the forms.
